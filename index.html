About Me

I'm a mother, a student and an entrepeneur who began a college career as a computer programmer but later ventured into the business world. After I completed a bachelors degree in business management and educational technology, I decided to go back to school to polish my skills as a web designer and developer. I've always had a passion for technology and I am glad I am able to dive into my passion and make it a career. One of the most beautiful things about technology and the world wide web is that I can be productive, efficient and resourceful from the comfort of my own home.
