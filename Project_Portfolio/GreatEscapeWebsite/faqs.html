FAQs

How much is it?

Pricing varies. Please click hereto see all the private rooms and their respective pricing.

How do I book a game?

Please book your game online on this website by clicking here.

Will I be playing with other participants?

No! Temporarily ALL games are PRIVATE experiences.

Do you offer Military discounts?

We do NOT offer a military discount at this time. Since COVID arrived we have made each room a PRIVATE experience therefore you are not required to purchase all of the seats. Because of this we have eliminated the offering of a military discount on top of the current discount of getting all of the seats a room holds.

How do I book an entire room?

ALL games are PRIVATE whether you buy all of the seats or not, however, there are a minimum number of tickets for each room that must be purchased whether you bring the minimum number or not. Visit each room to see maximum number of players.
